Winter Home Decor

Simple and easy!

What you need:

  • Grapevine from Afloral
  • Floral wire
  • Dried flowers from Afloral(The flower choice is up to you!)
  • The example has bunny tail, stardust, straw flowers, ferns in cream and immortelle!

    • Step One & Step Two

    Step One: Prepare your grapevine wreath but measuring the size you’d like your wreath to be, and securing the ends together with floral wire.

    Step Two: Start attaching your filler flower choice (like stardust) around your wreath in the pattern and fullness you prefer! Keep adding to create volume, and then move onto your other fillers, like ferns.

    Step Three

    Step Three:Finish filling in the wreath with a few bunny tails and immortelle. We finished our design with 2 straw flowers that we glued to the wreath (the heads break off easily so this is really the only way to use them in a wreath), and you’re all set!
