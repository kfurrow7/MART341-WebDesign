The 1950s

The 1950s A decade marked by the post-World War II boom, the dawn of the Cold War and the Civil Rights movement in the United States.
The 1950s: Drive-in[c] The drive-in really started to take off in the 50s. They offered family entertainment along with drive-ins offering more flexibility than indoor theaters. People could sit in their cars, they could bring their babies and they could smoke or drink.
